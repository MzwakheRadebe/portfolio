Bio

I build things that work.

I'm Mzwakhe Radebe, a software developer based in Mpumalanga, South Africa. I'm interested in the full spectrum of building software, from cloud infrastructure and backend systems to user-facing applications. I focus on writing clean, secure code and building things that actually work in the real world.

I've built and shipped real products — VitalAI, City Center Trade, and SiteSafe — all live and used by real users. That experience shaped how I think. Shipping matters more than chasing perfection on ideas that never leave your laptop.

I leverage AI to accelerate development and prototype rapidly, but my foundation is built on engineering rigor. I use tools to increase velocity, not to replace logic, I aim to understand each line of code.

I am expanding my backend expertise into cloud engineering, building a strong foundation in AWS. I approach full-stack development with a cloud-aware mindset, focusing on how applications are deployed, secured, and maintained in a live environment.
